  • In the Eternal Kingdoms, an Alliance is a group where players cooperate to share resources, strategies, and experiences. It is a family and community in your game.
  • Join or Create an Alliance: You can join an existing Alliance or create a new one. Both options offer benefits and unique cooperation opportunities.

  • Chat: Communicate with Alliance members through a dedicated chat feature. Only Alliance members can participate in this chat.
  • Technology: Contribute resources to upgrade Alliance Technology, benefiting all members. Technology requires Alliance Credits (AC) for research.
  • Alliance Help: Support other members by reducing Research, Building, and Healing times. This feature enhances cooperation and efficiency.
  • Rally Attacks: Organize coordinated attacks on Barbarian Forts, Enemy Cities, and other targets. Rally Attacks are an effective way to strategize with the Alliance.
  • Territory: Control areas on the map to receive resources. The more areas you control, the more resources you receive.
  • Support and Reinforce: Strengthen the Alliance's position by supporting and reinforcing allies in necessary situations.
  • Markers: Leaders and Officers can place markers to indicate important activities or guide members.
  • Shop: Use Alliance Credits to purchase rewards and items from the Alliance Shop.

  • Create an Alliance:
  • Open the main game dashboard and go to the Alliance section.
  • Click on "Create" and pay 500 gems.
  • Fill in the necessary information:
  • Alliance Tag: Choose an abbreviation for your Alliance.
  • Name: Provide the full name of the Alliance.
  • Announcement: Write a description or information about your Alliance.
  • Requirements: Set joining criteria (Anyone can join or Need Approval).
  • Language: Choose preferred languages for the Alliance.
  • Alliance Icon: Customize the Alliance Banner.
  • Invite members directly through the "Invite" feature in Alliance settings.
  • Join an Alliance:
  • Access the main dashboard and go to the Alliance section.
  • Click on "Join."
  • Browse available Alliances and apply or join directly.

  • Be Active: Participate regularly and help Alliance members.
  • Join Events: Participate in PVP and PVE events organized by the Alliance.
  • Support Allies: Strengthen and support teammates in critical situations.
  • Effective Communication: Be polite and constructive in conversations.
  • Contribute: Donate resources and help build Alliance structures.
  • Grow Together: Work to expand the Alliance and improve its capabilities.

  • Alliance Resource Center:
  • Provides resources for all members. Only one can be built at a time.
  • The Resource Center cannot be attacked, ensuring a continuous resource source.
  • Alliance Flag:
  • Marks and controls your territory on the map.
  • Flags are built from a Fortress and expand your influence.
  • Flags can be garrisoned and reinforced during attack times.
  • Maintain flags around your Alliance Fortress for better defense.
  • Efficient Alliance Flag Building:
  • Prioritize resource-rich areas.
  • Capture Alliance Resource Points to strengthen your position.
  • Ensure flags surround your Alliance Fortress for optimal defense.

The Alliance Fortress is an important structure and the main pillar of the Alliance that we must protect and respect at all times. Each Alliance can only build a maximum of 3 Fortresses. This is a necessary structure to perform the first TP of the game and organize collective activities within the Alliance.

  • Defense and Offense: The Fortress can be garrisoned by troops in times of war or peace, and can be continuously reinforced when under attack.
  • Only Leaders and Officers can repair: Only Leaders or Officers have the authority to repair the Fortress when needed.

  • Occupy Alliance Resource Nodes: Build Alliance Flags and occupy resource points within your Alliance territory.
  • Harvest Resources: Alliance members can gather resources within the Alliance territory. The Alliance will receive a portion of the resources from the members' harvesting.
  • How to earn Alliance Credits (Silver and Gold)?
  • Help the Alliance: Provide resources and support building, upgrading, researching technology, and healing troops to earn Alliance Credits daily (up to 10,000 per day).
  • Contribute to Alliance Technology: Contribute resources to Alliance Technology research to earn Alliance Credits.
  • Open Alliance Chests: Open gift chests from the Alliance to have the chance to receive Alliance Credits.
  • Construct Alliance Buildings: Help build Fortresses, Flags, or Resource Nodes to earn Alliance Credits.

  • Development: Enhance basic benefits for the Alliance such as maximum member count, building and research speed, and strengthen military power.
  • Territory: Enhance army attack, defense, and movement speed, as well as reinforce the durability of structures.
  • Warfare: Improve attack and defense capabilities of military units and enhance participation in coordinated attacks.
  • Alliance Skills: Provide special skills that Leaders and Officers can activate to benefit all members for a certain period.

Holy Sites: Special locations on the map that provide benefits such as increased attack, defense, building speed, and resource gathering for the Alliance. Occupying and building Holy Sites will provide these benefits to the Alliance and its members.

The City Hall is the center of all cities and crucial for development. Upgrading the City Hall helps unlock more features and increases the number of troops you can mobilize.

Upgrading the Academy provides military and economic buffs. This helps enhance the strength of the army and economic efficiency in the game.

The Trading Post is crucial due to tax rates. When upgraded to a higher level, the tax rate decreases to 8%, and you can transfer up to 5 million resources at once to allies (with a 100% transfer speed).

The VIP system in Eternal Kingdoms offers many benefits and buffs for your city. The higher the VIP level, the more privileges and advantages you receive.

The Scout Camp allows you to perform scouting actions. Besides clearing fog and discovering tribal villages or caves, scouting is crucial in warfare. When upgrading the Scout Camp, you can perform multiple scouting activities simultaneously and gain additional special abilities like improved movement speed and wider exploration range.

The Hospital is the only place to treat heavily wounded units in Eternal Kingdoms. When you engage in combat, heavily injured units are transferred to the hospital for treatment. You can have a maximum of 4 hospitals and do not need to upgrade all at once. Upgrade gradually to match the level of the City Hall. The Hospital determines your combat capability on the battlefield. If the hospital is full, you should not engage in combat as heavily injured troops will die immediately due to lack of space in the hospital.

The Wall is crucial, especially in the mid and late game. This is where you place defensive commanders. The Wall is the first line of defense for the city. If enemies want to plunder your city, they must pass through the wall first. Upgrading the Wall increases its durability. If the wall is breached, it will start burning, and its durability will decrease over time. When the durability reaches 0, you will be teleported to a random location. While the city is burning, you can extinguish it with Gems.
